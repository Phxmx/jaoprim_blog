# Image Placeholder Guide

## Required Images

Place your images in the following locations:

### Public Directory

- `/public/logo.png` or `/public/logo.jpg` - Your blog logo
- `/public/hero-image.jpg` - Hero section background image

### Photos (Home Page Highlights)

Place 6 photos in `/public/photos/`:

- photo1.jpg
- photo2.jpg
- photo3.jpg
- photo4.jpg
- photo5.jpg
- photo6.jpg

### Gallery (Gallery Page)

Place 12 photos in `/public/gallery/`:

- photo1.jpg through photo12.jpg

### Blog Posts

Place 2 images for each blog post in `/public/blog/`:

- before-china-1.jpg, before-china-2.jpg
- study-life-1.jpg, study-life-2.jpg
- travel-1.jpg, travel-2.jpg
- reflection-1.jpg, reflection-2.jpg

## Temporary Placeholders

For now, the site uses placeholder image paths. To add actual images:

1. Take photos from your ODOS experience
2. Rename them according to the structure above
3. Place them in the correct folders
4. The site will automatically display them

## Image Recommendations

- Hero image: 1920x1080px or larger
- Gallery photos: at least 1200px on the longest side
- Blog photos: at least 800px wide
- Logo: 200x200px PNG with transparent background
